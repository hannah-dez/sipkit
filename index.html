Our Story

SipKit started the same way most good ideas do: in a classroom, fueled by curiosity, caffeine, and a shared love for a really good cup of coffee. In January 2025, five entrepreneurship students came together with one simple question—what if getting into coffee didn't have to be complicated or intimidating?

We set out to make coffee feel more accessible, creative, and fun. SipKit brings together thoughtfully sourced coffee beans, custom 3D-printed grinders, and easy-to-use drip kits—everything you need to start brewing better coffee at home, your way.

But we didn't stop there. Through our online subscription, we share new recipes, brewing ideas, and hands-on ways to experiment with coffee beyond the basics. It's about learning, trying, and finding what your perfect cup looks like.

Our team of five brings something different to the table—management, 3D printing, marketing, finance, and web development—all working together to turn an idea into a growing brand. SipKit is built on collaboration, creativity, and the belief that great coffee should be approachable, not exclusive.
